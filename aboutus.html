About Us

The Christian Youth Ministry (CYM) of the Uniting Reformed Church in Southern Africa began as a small fellowship group and has grown into a youth-driven outreach programme. We provide mentorship, Bible studies, and community service projects such as food drives and skills development workshops.

Mission: To empower young people spiritually, emotionally, and socially by helping them build a strong foundation in God and by serving the needs of their communities.

Vision: To see a generation of youth rooted in faith, living with purpose, and transforming society through service and love.
