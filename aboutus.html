About CYM Botshabelo

Empowering young people through faith, service, and leadership.

Who We Are

CYM Botshabelo is a Christian youth organization under URCSA Botshabelo. We aim to spiritually uplift young people while helping them to grow socially, mentally, and emotionally. Our programs focus on leadership, community service, worship, and personal development.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build responsible, empowered, and spiritually grounded youth who can positively influence their community and the world.

Our Values

  • Faith & Spiritual Growth
  • Leadership Development
  • Community Service
  • Respect & Integrity
  • Youth Empowerment
